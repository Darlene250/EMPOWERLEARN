# EmpowerLearn

EmpowerLearn is a digital learning platform designed to help women build practical skills, grow confidence, and advance their careers through structured online learning.

## Project Overview

The platform combines accessible course discovery, guided learning journeys, and progress tracking in one experience. EmpowerLearn supports both individual learners and educators while giving administrators visibility into platform growth and engagement.

## Core Experience

- Secure social sign-in and onboarding flow
- Personalized learner profiles
- Course discovery with relevant video-based content
- Enrollment and progress tracking across courses
- Instructor tools for publishing and managing courses
- Admin tools for user and platform oversight

## Who It Serves

- Learners who want flexible, self-paced digital skill development
- Instructors who want to create and share educational content
- Program administrators who need to monitor participation and impact

## Product Goals

- Increase access to practical digital education
- Make learning progress visible and motivating
- Support role-based collaboration between learners, instructors, and admins
- Provide a scalable foundation for future learning programs and partnerships

## Tech Direction

EmpowerLearn is built as a modern full-stack web application with a dedicated frontend and backend, cloud-based authentication, and managed data services to support secure, reliable growth.

## Vision

EmpowerLearn aims to become a trusted learning ecosystem where women can continuously upskill, connect opportunity to learning, and move forward with confidence.
